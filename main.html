ABOUT CSE DEPARTMENT

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department typically offers a comprehensive study of computer science, technology, and engineering principles. The curriculum is designed to provide students with both theoretical and practical knowledge in areas like software development, systems engineering, and computer applications. The CSE department can vary slightly in focus depending on the university, but it generally covers a broad range of topics
Computer software engineers design and develop computer and video games, business applications and operating systems. Computer software engineers develop, design, and test software. Some software engineers design, construct, and maintain computer programs for companies.
